> That said, not every home needs the same level of prep. Some kitchens just need cleaning and minor touch-ups. Others benefit from a full cabinet refresh. We will tell you honestly what makes sense for your situation after we see the space.

**"How long does this take?"**
> Most kitchen and bath updates complete in 3-7 days depending on scope. Cabinet painting adds a few days for proper cure time. We coordinate around your listing timeline - if you have photos scheduled for next week, we plan backward from there.
>
> We understand 88% of sellers feel stressed about the selling process. We do not add to that stress. You get a clear timeline upfront and updates along the way.

**"Will I get my money back?"**

>
> More importantly, updated kitchens and baths reduce the chance of buyers asking for repair credits or price reductions. You are not just investing in higher value - you are protecting the value you already have.

**"What if I have a tight timeline?"**
> We get it. Sometimes you need to list fast - job relocation, estate situation, or just ready to move on. We have turned around kitchen updates in under a week when needed.
